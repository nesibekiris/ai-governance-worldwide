AI Regulations and Laws by Region - August 2025 Status

🇪🇺 European Union - EU AI Act

Status: Active law (Regulation 2024/1689)

Latest: GPAI obligations effective Aug 2, 2025

Approach: Risk-based with 4 tiers, 8 prohibited practices

Key Features: CE marking, conformity assessments, transparency

Penalties: Up to €35M or 7% global turnover (now active)

Timeline: Aug 2026 full application, Aug 2027 existing systems

Implementation: Only Spain, Denmark, Malta fully ready

🇺🇸 United States - AI Action Plan

Status: "Winning the AI Race" released July 23, 2025

Federal: EO 14148 & 14141 rescinded Biden oversight

Approach: 3 pillars - innovation, infrastructure, diplomacy

Key Features: 90 policy actions, federal site leasing

State Laws: CA (18 laws), NY RAISE Act, TX TRAIGA

Enforcement: FTC Operation AI Comply active

Penalties: Varies by state, FTC fines up to millions

🇨🇳 China - Multiple Active Regulations

Status: Continuous regulatory updates

Latest: AI labeling rules effective Sept 1, 2025

Key Laws: Algorithm Provisions, Deep Synthesis, GenAI Measures

Features: 1,400+ algorithms registered, security assessments

Enforcement: "Clear and Bright" campaign active

Penalties: Criminal up to 7 years, service suspension

Content: Socialist values alignment required

🇬🇧 United Kingdom - Pending AI Bill

Status: Non-statutory framework active

AI Bill: Lord Holmes' Bill expected Summer 2025

Approach: 5 principles via existing regulators

Features: No new AI regulator, sector-specific

Infrastructure: AI Growth Zones, 20x compute by 2030

Focus: Innovation and flexibility priority

🇯🇵 Japan - AI Promotion Act

Status: Law passed May 28, 2025

Philosophy: "Most AI-friendly country in the world"

Features: Zero penalties, voluntary principles

Enforcement: Name and shame only

Infrastructure: AI Strategy Center launching summer 2025

Partnership: SoftBank-OpenAI $3B annual deal

🇰🇷 South Korea - AI Framework Act

Status: Passed Dec 26, 2024, effective Jan 22, 2026

Approach: First comprehensive AI law in Asia-Pacific

Features: High-impact AI oversight, labeling requirements

Penalties: Administrative fines up to ₩30 million

Goals: Top 3 AI power by 2027, 70% deployment by 2030

Note: DeepSeek suspended for non-compliance

🇧🇷 Brazil - Pending AI Bill

Status: PL 2338/2023 passed Senate, in Chamber

Approach: Risk-based with human rights focus

Features: Excessive and high-risk categories

Penalties: Up to BRL 50M or 2% revenue

Timeline: Expected passage late 2025

Investment: BRL 23 billion AI plan

🇨🇦 Canada - Legislative Vacuum

Status: Bill C-27/AIDA died January 2025

Current: No federal AI legislation

Active: Canadian AI Safety Institute operational

Investment: CAD $2.4B Sovereign Compute Strategy

Future: Uncertain after October 2025 election

Approach: Research-first via CAISI
